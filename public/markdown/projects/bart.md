[Bartholomew](https://sr.ht/~siarune/bartholomew/) is an old summer vacation project where I made and hosted my own Discord bot.\
He _was_ hosted on Heroku, but I have since had to take him down after Heroku dropped their free tier.\
The original was built purely with discord.js and the minimal dependencies that entailed, although I'm considering rewriting the project with a few more bells and whistles- everything's deprecated at this point.\
If circumstanced permit, I might even host it myself and get a Discord developer license to put it up on the marketplace.
